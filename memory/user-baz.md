baz is a solo developer working on **RPGAtlas**, a browser-based 2D RPG-making engine that is his
**fork of `DriftwoodGaming/RPGAtlas`**. He contributes back upstream via PRs from feature branches on
his fork (`bazsupport/RPGAtlas`).

- Capable and hands-on: he runs and **validates everything in the browser himself**, and gives
  specific UX feedback ("the window's off-center", "the stick isn't shown") that should be treated as
  the spec. See [[validate-before-pr]].
- Works on **Windows**, across **multiple machines** (hence the repo-committed handoff on the
  `workspace` branch — see [[fork-and-config-policy]]). Also keeps other projects (several Godot games,
  ActionGameMaker, etc.) — their plans/memory are separate; don't mix them into RPGAtlas.
- Prefers direct, concise, technical collaboration: a recommendation over a menu, real trade-offs,
  no hand-holding.
